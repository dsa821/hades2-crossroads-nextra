</Boon>

### Sworn Flourish (Special) [#sworn-flourish]

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Hera.swornFlourish}>

- Special Damage: **+60%**

</Boon>

### Engagement Ring (Cast) [#engagement-ring]

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Hera.engagementRing}>

</Boon>

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Hera.nexusSprint}>

</Boon>

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Hera.bornGain}>

</Boon>
