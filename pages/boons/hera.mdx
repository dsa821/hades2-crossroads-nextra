# <IconHera className="mb-2" /> Hera

> The revered Queen of Olympus. She has more in common with us than one may first expect.\
> She ensures some level of order on Olympus is maintained, but there is only so much she can do. You wonder if you can help her.

<Box className="mt-4">
  **Hitch** _(Curse):_ Afflicted foes take **30%** of damage dealt to other
  afflicted foes. Lasts **8 Sec.**
</Box>

</BoonTest>

### Sworn Flourish (Special) [#sworn-flourish]

<BoonTest boon={DbBoons.Hera.swornFlourish}>

</BoonTest>

### Engagement Ring (Cast) [#engagement-ring]

<BoonTest boon={DbBoons.Hera.engagementRing}>

</BoonTest>

### Nexus Sprint (Sprint) [#nexus-sprint]

<BoonTest boon={DbBoons.Hera.nexusSprint}>

</BoonTest>

<BoonTest boon={DbBoons.Hera.bornGain}>

</BoonTest>
