# Hera

> The revered Queen of Olympus. She has more in common with us than one may
> first expect.\
> She ensures some level of order on Olympus is maintained, but there is only so
> much she can do. You wonder if you can help her.

<Boon className="mt-4">
  **Hitch** _(Curse):_ Afflicted foes take **30%** of damage dealt to other
  afflicted foes. Lasts **8 Sec.**
</Boon>

## Core

### Sworn Strike (Attack) [#sworn-strike]

<Boon element="earth">
Your **Attacks** deal more damage and inflict **Hitch**.

- Attack Damage: **+50%**

</Boon>

### Sworn Flourish (Special) [#sworn-flourish]

<Boon element="earth">
Your **Specials** deal more damage and inflict **Hitch**.

- Special Damage: **+60%**

</Boon>

### Engagement Ring (Cast) [#engagement-ring]

<Boon element="earth">
Your **Casts** last **200%** longer and deal damage to _every_ foe that joins the **Encounter**.

- On-Spawn Damage: **70**

</Boon>

### Nexus Sprint (Sprint) [#nexus-sprint]

<Boon element="earth">
Your **Sprint** inflicts **Hitch** on nearby foes, which spreads to other foes near _them_.

- Additional Foes Hitched: **+1**

</Boon>

### Born Gain (Magick) [#born-gain]

<Boon element="earth">
Whenever you run out of <IconMagick />, **Prime** to restore _all_ <IconMagick /> up to the reduced limit.

- Magick Primed: **10**

</Boon>

### Proper Upbringing (Infusion) [#proper-upbringing]

<Boon>
While you have at least **3** <IconEarth />, _all_ your **Common Boons** gain **Rarity**.

- Improved Rarity: **Rare**

</Boon>

**Required: 1 Earth**

## Support

### Bridal Glow

<Boon element="earth">
Your **Boons** become **Heroic**, then lose **Rarity** every **5 Encounters**.

- Random Boons Affected: **2**

</Boon>

### Uncommon Grace

<Boon  element="earth">
While none of your other **Boons** are **Common**, deal more damage.

- Common-Less Bonus Damage: **+10%**

</Boon>

### Nasty Comeback

<Boon element="earth">
After you take damage, inflict your foe with **Hitch** and deal damage in greater measure.

- Damage Reflected: **500%**

</Boon>

### Hereditary Bane

<Boon element="earth">

</Boon>
