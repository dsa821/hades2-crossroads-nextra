# Poseidon

> Your boisterous uncle, sometimes called the Earthshaker, although he is best
> known for the seas which smother much of the surface.\
> He is comfortable in adverse situations, and quite unable to hold still much like
> the seas themselves.

<Boon className="mt-4">
  **Slip** _(Curse):_ Afflicted foes take more damage and are knocked farther
  away. Lasts **3 Sec.**
</Boon>

## Core

### Wave Strike (Attack) [#wave-strike]

<Boon element="water">
Your **Attacks** hit foes with a splash that knocks other foes away.

- Splash Damage: **15**

</Boon>

### Wave Flourish (Special) [#wave-flourish]

<Boon element="water">
Your **Specials** hit foes with a splash that knocks other foes away.

- Splash Damage: **20**

</Boon>

### Geyser Ring (Cast) [#geyser-ring]

<Boon element="water">
Your **Î© Cast** immediately detonates, dealing damage and knocking foes away.

- Omega Cast Damage: **100**

</Boon>

### Breaker Sprint (Sprint) [#breaker-sprint]

<Boon element="water">
Your **Sprint** deals damage on impact and knocks foes away, but uses **5** <IconMagick /> per hit.

- Impact Damage: **80**

</Boon>

### Fluid Gain (Magick) [#fluid-gain]

<Boon element="water">
After you strike foes with your **Weapon**, a **Spirit Bubble** may appear.

- Spirit Bubble Chance: **10%**

**Spirit Bubble**: Restores **20** <IconMagick /> when you touch it. Expires
when used, or after **30 Sec.**

</Boon>

### Water Fitness (Infusion) [#water-fitness]

<Boon>
While you have at least **4** <IconWater />, gain Max Life.

- Max Life: **+100**

</Boon>
**Required: 1 Water**

## Support

### Double Up

<Boon element="water">
Whenever you claim a **Minor Find** or similar resource reward, a copy may appear.

- Double Reward Chance: **20%**

</Boon>

### Hydraulic Might

<Boon element="water">
At the start of each **Encounter**, your **Attacks** and **Specials** are stronger for **10 Sec.**

- Initial Damage Bonus: **+100%**

</Boon>

### Flood Control (Prime) [#flood-control]

<Boon element="water">
After you enter a **Location**, **Prime 30** <IconMagick /> to reduce any damage you would take.

- Damage Reduction per Hit: **-2**

</Boon>

### Sunken Treasure

<Boon element="water">
Gain <IconGold />, <IconHealing />, and sometimes <IconAshes /> and <IconPsyche />.

- Gold Received: **+90**

</Boon>

### Ocean's Bounty

<Boon element="water">
Any **Minor Finds** and <IconGold /> are worth more.

- Reward Value: **+50%**

</Boon>

### Slippery Slope

<Boon element="water">
Your splash effects from **Poseidon** also inflict **Slip** on foes.

- Slip Bonus Damage: **+5%**

</Boon>

**Required** _(one of the following):_

- [Wave Strike](#wave-strike)
- [Wave Flourish](#wave-flourish)

### Crashing Wave

<Boon element="water">
Whenever your knock-away effects slam foes into barriers, create a blast in the area.

- Blast Damage: **50**

</Boon>

**Required** _(one of the following):_

- [Wave Strike](#wave-strike)
- [Wave Flourish](#wave-flourish)
- [Geyser Ring](#geyser-ring)
- [Breaker Sprint](#breaker-sprint)
