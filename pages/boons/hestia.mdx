# Hestia

> The goddess of the hearth is very wise, inasmuch as she altogether avoids most matters of familial politics. But there are some matters in which even she must become involved.\
> She likes you, and you like her. She does not stand on ceremony. You could learn a thing or two of that.

<Boon className='mt-4'>
  **Scorch** _(Curse):_ Afflicted foes take **40** damage accrued from this
  every **1 Sec.**
</Boon>

## Core

### Flame Strike (Attack)

<Boon>
Your **Attacks** inflict **Scorch**.

- Scorch Damage: **20**

</Boon>

### Flame Flourish (Special)

<Boon>
Your **Specials** inflict **Scorch**.

- Scorch Damage: **15**

</Boon>

### Smolder Ring (Cast)

<Boon>
Your **Casts** repeatedly inflict **Scorch** on foes.

- Scorch Damage: **30** _(every 1 Sec.)_

</Boon>

### Soot Sprint (Sprint)

<Boon>
Your **Sprint** destroys most ranged shots near you, and inflicts **Scorch** on foes that fired.

- Scorch Damage per Projectile: **2**

</Boon>

### Hearth Gain (Magick)

<Boon>
Rapidly restore **Magick**, but you have **-20% Max Life**

- Magick Restoration: **7** _(every 1 Sec.)_

</Boon>

### Slow Cooker (Infusion)

<Boon>
Your **Attacks** and **Specials** gain **Power** for each **Fire** you have.

- Power per Fire Boon: **+2**

</Boon>

**Required: 1 Fire**

## Support

### Glowing Coal

<Boon>
Hold **Cast** to aim a fiery projectile that explodes on impact. The binding circle forms there.

- Blast Damage: **50**

</Boon>

### Controlled Burn

<Boon>
Your **Ω Special** also launches a fiery projectile, but uses **+10 Magick**

- Blast Damage: **80**

</Boon>

### Burnt Offering

<Boon>
Gain **Max Life** and **Max Magick**, but give up **1 Boon** selected by **Hestia**.

- Max Life & Magick: **+50**

</Boon>

### Flammable Coating

<Boon>
Your **Scorch** effects deal bonus damage to **Armor**.

- Scorch Damage vs. Armor: **+100%**

</Boon>

**Required** _(one of the following):_

- Flame Strike
- Flame Flourish
- Smolder Ring
- Spontaneous Combustion

### Fire Extinguisher

<Boon>
Foes with at least **300 Scorch** take a burst of damage that consumes the effect.

- Damage from Scorch: **50%**

</Boon>

**Required** _(one of the following):_

- Flame Strike
- Flame Flourish
- Smolder Ring
- Spontaneous Combustion

### Natural Gas

<Boon>
Whenever **Scorch**-afflicted foes are slain, they damage nearby foes.

- Blast Damage: **60**

</Boon>

**Required** _(one of the following):_

- Flame Strike
- Flame Flourish
- Smolder Ring
- Spontaneous Combustion

### Spontaneous Combustion

<Boon>
Your **Ω Special** inflicts bonus **Scorch** if foes are unafflicted.

- Scorch Damage: **60**

</Boon>

**Required**:

- Flame Flourish
