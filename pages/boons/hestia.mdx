# <IconHestia className="mb-2" /> Hestia

> The goddess of the hearth is very wise, inasmuch as she altogether avoids most
> matters of familial politics. But there are some matters in which even she
> must become involved.\
> She likes you, and you like her. She does not stand on ceremony. You could learn
> a thing or two of that.

<Boon className="mt-4">
  **Scorch** _(Curse):_ Afflicted foes take **40** damage accrued from this
  every **1 Sec.**
</Boon>

## Core

### Flame Strike (Attack) [#flame-strike]

<Boon element="fire">
Your **Attacks** inflict **Scorch**.

- Scorch Damage: **20**

</Boon>

### Flame Flourish (Special) [#flame-flourish]

<Boon element="fire">
Your **Specials** inflict **Scorch**.

- Scorch Damage: **15**

</Boon>

### Smolder Ring (Cast) [#smolder-ring]

<Boon element="fire">
Your **Casts** repeatedly inflict **Scorch** on foes.

- Scorch Damage: **30** _(every 1 Sec.)_

</Boon>

### Soot Sprint (Sprint) [#soot-sprint]

<Boon element="fire">
Your **Sprint** destroys most ranged shots near you, and inflicts **Scorch** on foes that fired.

- Scorch Damage per Projectile: **2**

</Boon>

### Hearth Gain (Magick) [#hearth-gain]

<Boon element="fire">
Rapidly restore <IconMagick />, but you have **-20%** <IconMaxLife />.

- Magick Restoration: **7** _(every 1 Sec.)_

</Boon>

### Slow Cooker (Infusion) [#slow-cooker]

<Boon>
Your **Attacks** and **Specials** gain **Power** for each <IconFire /> you have.

- Power per Fire Boon: **+2**

</Boon>

**Required: 1 Fire**
