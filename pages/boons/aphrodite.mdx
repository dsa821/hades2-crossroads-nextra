## Core

### Flutter Strike (Attack) [#flutter-strike]

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Aphrodite.flutterStrike}>
Your **Attacks** deal more damage to nearby foes.

- Close-Up Damage: **+80%**

</Boon>

### Flutter Flourish (Special) [#flutter-flourish]

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Aphrodite.flutterFlourish}>
Your **Specials** deal more damage to nearby foes.

- Close-Up Damage: **+100%**

</Boon>

### Rapture Ring (Cast) [#rapture-ring]

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Aphrodite.raptureRing}>
Your **Casts** inflict **Weak**, and damage foes while dragging them toward the center.

- Cast Damage: **10** _(every 0.85 Sec.)_

</Boon>

### Passion Rush (Dash) [#passion-rush]

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Aphrodite.passionRush}>
**Dashing** damages surrounding foes and inflicts **Weak**, and again once you stop.

- Area Damage: **20**

</Boon>

### Glamour Gain (Magick) [#glamour-gain]

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Aphrodite.glamourGain}>
You inflict **Weak** on nearby foes, and gradually restore <IconMagick /> while any nearby foe is **Weak**.

- Magick Restoration: **6** _(every 1 Sec.)_

</Boon>

## Support

### <TextHighlight type="infusion">Wispy Wiles (Infusion)</TextHighlight> [#wispy-wiles]

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Aphrodite.wispyWiles}>
Gain a chance to **Dodge** for each <IconAir /> you have.

- Dodge Chance per Air Boon: **+3%**

</Boon>

<Callout>
  **Required: 2** <IconAir />
</Callout>

### Secret Crush

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Aphrodite.secretCrush}>
After you enter a **Location**, **Prime 20** <IconMagick /> to add **Power** to your **Attack**.

- Attack Power: **+5%**

</Boon>

### Life Affirmation

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Aphrodite.lifeAffirmation}>
Any <IconMaxLife /> rewards you find have a greater effect.

- Bonus Life Gain: **+40%**

</Boon>

### Shameless Attitude

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Aphrodite.shamelessAttitude}>
You deal more damage. While you have at least **80%** <IconLife />, the bonus is doubled.

- High-Life Bonus Damage: **+10%**

</Boon>

### Heart Breaker

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Aphrodite.heartBreaker}>
Whenever you use **30** <IconMagick />, create a **Heartthrob**.

- Heartthrob Area Damage: **120**

**Heartthrobs**: Explosive projectiles that revolves around you. Expire after
**20 Sec.**

</Boon>

### Healthy Rebound

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Aphrodite.healthyRebound}>
Whenever you exit a **Location**, restore **100%** <IconLife /> if you have not lost too much.

- Min Life Required: **80%**

</Boon>

<Callout>
  **Required**:
  <BoonGrid boons={[DbBoons.Aphrodite.shamelessAttitude]} />
</Callout>

### Broken Resolve

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Aphrodite.brokenResolve}>
Your **Weak** effects are more potent.

- Weak Damage Reduction: **+10%**

</Boon>

<Callout>
  **Required** _(one of the following):_
  <BoonGrid
    boons={[
      DbBoons.Aphrodite.raptureRing,
      DbBoons.Aphrodite.passionRush,
      DbBoons.Aphrodite.glamourGain,
    ]}
  />
</Callout>

### Sweet Surrender

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Aphrodite.sweetSurrender}>
**Weak**-afflicted foes take more damage.

- Damage vs. Weak: **+10%**

</Boon>

<Callout>
  **Required** _(one of the following):_
  <BoonGrid
    boons={[
      DbBoons.Aphrodite.raptureRing,
      DbBoons.Aphrodite.passionRush,
      DbBoons.Aphrodite.glamourGain,
    ]}
  />
</Callout>
