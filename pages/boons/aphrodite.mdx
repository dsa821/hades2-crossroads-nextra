# <IconAphrodite className="mb-2" /> Aphrodite

> You have much to learn from her, at some point.\
> She is more than she appears. For instance, she is much older than most of the
> others on her mountain, yet holds fast to her carefree look and spirit.

<Boon className="mt-4">
  **Weak** _(Curse):_ Afflicted foes deal at least **10%** less damage. Lasts
  **3 Sec.**
</Boon>

## Core

### Flutter Strike (Attack) [#flutter-strike]

<Boon element="water">
Your **Attacks** deal more damage to nearby foes.

- Close-Up Damage: **+80%**

</Boon>

### Flutter Flourish (Special) [#flutter-flourish]

<Boon element="water">
Your **Specials** deal more damage to nearby foes.

- Close-Up Damage: **+100%**

</Boon>

### Rapture Ring (Cast) [#rapture-ring]

<Boon element="air">
Your **Casts** drag foes in and inflict **Weak**.

- Weak Damage Reduction: **+10%**

</Boon>

### Passion Dash (Dash) [#passion-dash]

<Boon element="air">
Your **Dash** blasts foes near where you start and end, and inflicts **Weak**.

- Blast Damage: **20**

</Boon>

### Glamour Gain (Magick) [#glamour-gain]

<Boon element="air">
In each **Encounter**, **1** foe is always **Weak**. You gradually restore <IconMagick /> near **Weak** foes.

- Magick Restoration: **6** _(every 1 Sec.)_

</Boon>

### Wispy Wiles (Infusion) [#wispy-wiles]

<Boon>
While you have at least **4** <IconAir />, you may **Dodge** any damage.

- Dodge Chance: **+15%**

</Boon>

**Required: 1 Air**

## Support

### Healthy Rebound

<Boon element="water">
Whenever you exit a **Location**, restore **100%** <IconLife /> if you have not lost too much.

- Min Life Required: **80%**

</Boon>

### Secret Crush

<Boon element="air">
After you enter a **Location**, **Prime 20** <IconMagick /> to add **Power** to your **Attack**.

- Attack Power: **+5%**

</Boon>

### Life Affirmation

<Boon element="air">
Any <IconMaxLife /> rewards you find have a greater effect.

- Bonus Life Gain: **+40%**

</Boon>

### Shameless Attitude

<Boon element="air">
While you have at least **80%** <IconLife />, you deal more damage.

- High-Life Bonus Damage: **+10%**

</Boon>

### Heart Breaker

<Boon element="water">
Whenever you use **30** <IconMagick />, create a **Heartthrob**.

- Heartthrob Area Damage: **120**

**Heartthrobs**: Explosive projectiles that revolves around you. Expire after
**20 Sec.**

</Boon>

### Broken Resolve

<Boon element="water">
Your **Weak** effects are more potent.

- Weak Damage Reduction: **+10%**

</Boon>

**Required** _(one of the following):_

- [Rapture Ring](#rapture-ring)
- [Passion Dash](#passion-dash)
- [Glamour Gain](#glamour-gain)

### Sweet Surrender

<Boon element="water">
**Weak**-afflicted foes take more damage.

- Damage vs. Weak: **+10%**

</Boon>

**Required** _(one of the following):_

- [Rapture Ring](#rapture-ring)
- [Passion Dash](#passion-dash)
- [Glamour Gain](#glamour-gain)
