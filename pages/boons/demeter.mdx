# Demeter

> Your mother once fled from her, such is her doting and particular nature, our great goddess of seasons.\
> Her long life has fueled in her a particular king of wrath that is feared even on her mountain. It is fortunate she likes you.

<Boon className='mt-4'>
  **Freeze** _(Curse):_ Afflicted foes are stopped for **2 Sec.** Can be
  re-applied after **10 Sec.** Lasts **8 Sec.**
</Boon>

## Core

### Ice Strike (Attack) [#ice-strike]

<Boon element="water">
Your **Attacks** deal more damage and inflict **Freeze**.

- Attack Damage: **+30%**

</Boon>

### Ice Flourish (Special) [#ice-flourish]

<Boon element="water">
Your **Specials** deal more damage and inflict **Freeze**.

- Special Damage: **+40%**

</Boon>

### Arctic Ring (Cast) [#arctic-ring]

<Boon element="water">
Your **Casts** repeatedly deal damage in the area and inflict **Freeze**.

- Cast Damage: **10** _(every 0.5 Sec.)_

</Boon>

### Frigid Sprint (Sprint) [#frigid-sprint]

<Boon element="water">
Your **Sprint** forms a **Cyclone** around you that lingers after you stop.

- Cyclone Damage: **4** _(every 0.25 Sec.)_

**Cyclone**: A vortex that makes foes **20%** slower and their ranged shots **60%** slower. Lasts **3 Sec.**

</Boon>

### Tranquil Gain (Magick) [#tranquil-gain]

<Boon element="earth">
After remaining inactive for **1 Sec.**, rapidly restore **Magick** until you act.

- Magick Restoration: **+25** _(every 1 Sec.)_

</Boon>

### Coarse Grit (Infusion) [#coarse-grit]

<Boon>
While you have at least **6** <IconElement element="earth" />, you cannot take more damage per hit than the limit.

- Max Damage per Hit: **15**

</Boon>

**Required: 1 Earth**

## Support

### Rare Crop

<Boon element="earth">
Your **Boons** become **Common**, then gain **Rarity** every **3 Encounters**.

- Random Boons Affected: **1**

</Boon>

### Local Climate

<Boon element="earth">
Your **Ω Cast** deals bonus damage and follows you, even as you start to **Channel** it.

- Cast Damage: **+20%**

</Boon>

### Gale Force

<Boon element="water">
Your **Casts** also create a **Cyclone** at the binding circle.

- Cyclone Damage: **4** _(every 0.25 Sec.)_

**Cyclone**: A vortex that makes foes **20%** slower and their ranged shots **60%** slower. Lasts **3 Sec.**

</Boon>

### Plentiful Forage

<Boon element="earth">
Whenever you gather plants, seeds, or mushrooms, gain **Max Life**. Receive **1 Mystery Seed** now.

- Max Life from Gathering: **5**

</Boon>

### Winter Coat (Prime) [#winter-coat]

<Boon element="earth">
After you enter a **Location**, **Prime** a barrier that absorbs **1** instance of damage.

- Magick Primed: **20**

</Boon>

### Weed Killer

<Boon element="earth">
Your **Ω Attack** deals more damage, but uses **+10 Magick**.

- Omega Attack Damage: **+50%**

</Boon>

### Cold Storage

<Boon element="water">
Your **Freeze** effects last longer.

- Freeze Duration: **+ 2 Sec.**

</Boon>

**Required** _(one of the following):_

- Ice Strike
- Ice Flourish
