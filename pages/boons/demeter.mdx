# <GreekTitle icon={<IconDemeter  />} name="Demeter" />

> Your mother once fled from her, such is her doting and particular nature, our
> great goddess of seasons.\
> Her long life has fueled in her a particular king of wrath that is feared even
> on her mountain. It is fortunate she likes you.

<Box className="mt-4">

</Box>

## Core

### Ice Strike (Attack) [#ice-strike]

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Demeter.iceStrike}>
Your **Attacks** deal more damage and inflict **Freeze**.

- Attack Damage: **+30%**

</Boon>

### Ice Flourish (Special) [#ice-flourish]

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Demeter.iceFlourish}>
Your **Specials** deal more damage and inflict **Freeze**.

- Special Damage: **+40%**

</Boon>

### Arctic Ring (Cast) [#arctic-ring]

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Demeter.arcticRing}>
Your **Casts** repeatedly deal damage in the area and inflict **Freeze**.

- Cast Damage: **10** _(every 0.5 Sec.)_

</Boon>

### Frigid Sprint (Sprint) [#frigid-sprint]

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Demeter.frigidSprint}>
Your **Sprint** forms a **Cyclone** around you that lingers after you stop.

- Cyclone Damage: **4** _(every 0.25 Sec.)_

**Cyclone**: A vortex that makes foes **20%** slower and their ranged shots
**60%** slower. Lasts **3 Sec.**

</Boon>

### Tranquil Gain (Magick) [#tranquil-gain]

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Demeter.tranquilGain}>
After remaining inactive for **1 Sec.**, rapidly restore <IconMagick /> until you act.

- Magick Restoration: **+25** _(every 1 Sec.)_

</Boon>

## Support

### <TextHighlight type="infusion">Coarse Grit (Infusion)</TextHighlight> [#coarse-grit]

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Demeter.coarseGrit}>
While you have at least **6** <IconEarth />, you cannot take more damage per hit than the limit.

- Max Damage per Hit: **15**

</Boon>

<Callout>
  **Required: 1** <IconEarth />
</Callout>

### Rare Crop

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Demeter.rareCrop}>
Your **Boons** become **Common**, then gain **Rarity** every **3 Encounters**.

- Random Boons Affected: **1**

</Boon>

### Local Climate

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Demeter.localClimate}>
Your **Ω Cast** deals bonus damage and follows you, even as you start to **Channel** it.

- Cast Damage: **+20%**

</Boon>

### Gale Force

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Demeter.galeForce}>
Your **Casts** also create a **Cyclone** at the binding circle.

- Cyclone Damage: **4** _(every 0.25 Sec.)_

**Cyclone**: A vortex that makes foes **20%** slower and their ranged shots
**60%** slower. Lasts **3 Sec.**

</Boon>

### Plentiful Forage

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Demeter.plentifulForage}>
Whenever you gather plants, seeds, or mushrooms, gain <IconMaxLife />. Receive **1** <IconMysterySeed /> now.

- Max Life from Gathering: **5**

</Boon>

### Winter Coat (Prime) [#winter-coat]

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Demeter.winterCoat}>
After you enter a **Location**, **Prime** a barrier that absorbs **1** instance of damage.

- Magick Primed: **20**

</Boon>

### Weed Killer

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Demeter.weedKiller}>
Your **Ω Attack** deals more damage, but uses **+10** <IconMagick />.

- Omega Attack Damage: **+50%**

</Boon>

### Cold Storage

<Boon boon={DbBoons.Demeter.coldStorage}>
Your **Freeze** effects last longer.

- Freeze Duration: **+ 2 Sec.**

</Boon>

<Callout>
  **Required** _(one of the following):_
