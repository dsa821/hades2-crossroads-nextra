# <IconApollo className="mb-1" /> Apollo

> A god in the guise of a beautiful youth. You are left to conclude that his
> genial manner toward you is, in fact, sincere.\
> Conflict is not really in his nature, thus he is often out of his depth, despite
> how well he gets on anyway.

<Boon className="mt-4">
  **Daze** _(Curse):_ Afflicted foes have **20%** chance to deal no damage.
  Lasts **9 Sec.**
</Boon>

## Core

### Nova Strike (Attack) [#nova-strike]

<Boon element="air">
Your **Attacks** deal more damage in a larger area.

- Attack Damage: **+40%**

</Boon>

### Nova Flourish (Special) [#nova-flourish]

<Boon element="air">
Your **Specials** deal more damage in a larger area.

- Special Damage: **+60%**

</Boon>

### Solar Ring (Cast) [#solar-ring]

<Boon element="fire">
After your **Ω Cast** expires, rapidly deal damage in the area for **2 Sec.**

- Omega Cast Damage: **10** _(every 0.13 Sec.)_

</Boon>

### Blinding Sprint (Sprint) [#blinding-sprint]

<Boon element="fire">
Your **Sprint** is faster and inflicts **Daze** on nearby foes.

- Sprint Speed: **+30%**

</Boon>

### Lucid Gain (Magick) [#lucid-gain]

<Boon element="air">
While standing in your **Casts**, gradually restore <IconMagick />.

- Magick Restoration: **+12** _(every 1 Sec.)_

</Boon>

### Self Healing (Infusion) [#self-healing]

<Boon >
While you have at least **3** <IconFire />, whenever you take damage, restore some <IconLife />.

- Damage Recovered: **30%** _(over 5 Sec.)_

</Boon>
**Required: 1 Fire**

## Support

### Super Nova

<Boon element="air">
Your **Casts** expand in size until they expire.

- Max Cast Size: **+40%**

</Boon>

### Light Smite

<Boon element="fire">
After you take damage, your foe takes damage and you inflict **Daze** on _all_ foes.

- Revenge Damage: **50**

</Boon>

### Extra Dose

<Boon element="fire">
Your **Attack** has a chance to hit **2** times.

- Double Strike Chance: **+5%**

</Boon>

### Perfect Image

<Boon element="air">
In each **Encounter**, you deal more damage until you take damage.

- No-Hit Bonus Damage: **+10%**

</Boon>

### Back Burner

<Boon element="fire">
Foes with **Daze** take more damage if struck from behind.

- Backstab Damage: **+50%**

</Boon>

**Required**: _(one of the following):_

- [Blinding Sprint](#blinding-sprint)
- [Light Smite](#light-smite)
- [Dazzling Display](#dazzling-display)
