# Apollo

> A god in the guise of a beautiful youth. You are left to conclude that his genial manner toward you is, in fact, sincere.\
> Conflict is not really in his nature, thus he is often out of his depth, despite how well he gets on anyway.

<Boon className='mt-4'>
  **Daze** _(Curse):_ Afflicted foes have **20%** chance to deal no damage. Lasts **9 Sec.**
</Boon>

## Core

### Nova Strike (Attack)

<Boon>
Your **Attacks** deal more damage in a larger area.

- Attack Damage: **+40%**

</Boon>

### Nova Flourish (Special)

<Boon>
Your **Specials** deal more damage in a larger area.

- Special Damage: **+60%**

</Boon>

### Solar Ring (Cast)

<Boon>
After your **Ω Cast** expires, rapidly deal damage in the area for **2 Sec.**

- Omega Cast Damage: **10** _(every 0.13 Sec.)_

</Boon>

### Blinding Sprint (Sprint)

<Boon>
Your **Sprint** is faster and inflicts **Daze** on nearby foes.

- Sprint Speed: **+30%**

</Boon>

### Lucid Gain (Magick)

<Boon>
While standing in your **Casts**, gradually restore **Magick**.

- Magick Restoration: **+12** _(every 1 Sec.)_

</Boon>

### Self Healing (Infusion)

<Boon>
While you have at least **3 Fire**, whenever you take damage, restore some **Life**.

- Damage Recovered: **30%** _(over 5 Sec.)_

</Boon>
**Required: 1 Fire**

## Support

### Super Nova

<Boon>
Your **Casts** expand in size until they expire.

- Max Cast Size: **+40%**

</Boon>

### Light Smite

<Boon>
After you take damage, your foe takes damage and you inflict **Daze** on _all_ foes.

- Revenge Damage: **50**

</Boon>

### Extra Dose

<Boon>
Your **Attack** has a chance to hit **2** times.

- Double Strike Chance: **+5%**

</Boon>

### Perfect Image

<Boon>
In each **Encounter**, you deal more damage until you take damage.

- No-Hit Bonus Damage: **+10%**

</Boon>

### Back Burner

<Boon>
Foes with **Daze** take more damage if struck from behind.

- Backstab Damage: **+50%**

</Boon>

**Required**: _(one of the following):_

- Blinding Sprint
- Light Smite
- Dazzling Display

### Critical Miss

<Boon>
Foes take damage whenever **Daze** causes them to miss.

- Miss Damage: **100**

</Boon>

**Required**: _(one of the following):_

- Blinding Sprint
- Light Smite
- Dazzling Display

### Dazzling Display

<Boon>
Your **Attacks** may inflict **Daze**.

- Daze Chance: **+10%**

</Boon>

**Required**:

- Nova Strike
