# <IconHephaestus /> Hephaestus

> You do not know what to make of this one. He rejects our craft, believes only in what he can grasp with his thick hands.\
> But he is a recluse in his own realm. In that you feel a connection.

<Box className="mt-4">
  **Vent** _(Curse):_ After **5 Sec.**, afflicted foes take a burst of damage.
  Timer resets if re-applied.
</Box>

## Core

### Volcanic Strike (Attack) [#volcanic-strike]

<BoonTest boon={DbBoons.Hephaestus.volcanicStrike}>
Your **Attacks** occasionally create a blast that deals **200** damage in the area.

- Blast Recharge Time: **12 Sec.**

</BoonTest>

### Volcanic Flourish (Special) [#volcanic-flourish]

<BoonTest boon={DbBoons.Hephaestus.volcanicFlourish}>
Your **Specials** occasionally create a blast that deals **400** damage in the area.

- Blast Recharge Time: **+20 Sec.**

</BoonTest>

### Anvil Ring (Cast) [#anvil-ring]

<BoonTest boon={DbBoons.Hephaestus.anvilRing}>
Your **Casts** deal damage **3** times in succession, but in a smaller area.

- Cast Damage: **50** _(every 1 Sec.)_

</BoonTest>

### Smithy Sprint (Sprint) [#smithy-sprint]

<BoonTest boon={DbBoons.Hephaestus.smithySprint}>
After you **Sprint** for **1 Sec.**, use **10** <IconMagick /> to create a blast that deals damage to nearby foes.

- Area Damage: **200**

</BoonTest>

### Fixed Gain (Magick) [#fixed-gain]

<BoonTest boon={DbBoons.Hephaestus.fixedGain}>
You take **-10%** damage, and restore some <IconMagick /> whenever you take damage.

- Magick Restored from Damage: **50**

</BoonTest>

## Support

### <TextHighlight type="infusion">Martial Art (Infusion)</TextHighlight> [#martial-art]

<BoonTest boon={DbBoons.Hephaestus.martialArt}>
Your **Attack** and **Special** deal more damage for each <IconEarth /> you have.

- Damage per Earth Boon: **+5%**

</BoonTest>

<Callout>
  **Required: 1** <IconEarth />
</Callout>

### Molten Touch

<BoonTest boon={DbBoons.Hephaestus.moltenTouch}>
Your **Attacks** and **Specials** deal bonus damage to **Armor**.

- Damage vs. Armor: **+20%**

</BoonTest>

### Trusty Shield

<BoonTest boon={DbBoons.Hephaestus.trustyShield}>
After you enter a **Location**, **Prime 30** <IconMagick /> to gain <IconArmor /> until the next **Location**.

- Starting Armor: **+10**

</BoonTest>

### Tough Trade

<BoonTest boon={DbBoons.Hephaestus.toughTrade}>
If you take damage during your **Attacks** or **Specials**, they are stronger if they hit.

- Bonus Weapon Damage: **+100%**

</BoonTest>

### Mint Condition

<BoonTest boon={DbBoons.Hephaestus.mintCondition}>
At the start of each **Encounter**, you are briefly **Impervious**.

- Impervious Duration: **8 Sec.**

</BoonTest>

### Heavy Metal

<BoonTest boon={DbBoons.Hephaestus.heavyMetal}>
Gain some <IconArmor />. Foes' attacks cannot knock you away.

- Armor Gained: **+50**

</BoonTest>

### Uncanny Fortitude

<BoonTest boon={DbBoons.Hephaestus.uncannyFortitude}>
Gain bonus <IconMaxLife /> based on your <IconMagick /> limit.

- Max Life from Magick: **+20%**

</BoonTest>

### Furnace Blast

<BoonTest boon={DbBoons.Hephaestus.furnaceBlast}>
Your blast effects from **Hephaestus** also inflict **Vent** on foes.

- Vent Damage: **300**

</BoonTest>
