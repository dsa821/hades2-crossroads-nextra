# Arcana Cards

<Callout>All values given are for rank 3.</Callout>

## Row 1

### The Sorceress [#sorceress]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.sorceress}>
  While you **Channel** your **Ω Moves**, everything moves slower for **4 Sec.**
</Arcana>

### The Wayward Son [#wayward-son]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.waywardSon}>
  After you exit a **Location**, restore **4** <IconLife />, or twice that if
  you have no more than **30%** <IconLife />.
</Arcana>

### The Huntress [#huntress]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.huntress}>
  While you have less than **100%** <IconMagick />, your **Attack** and
  **Special** deal **+50%** damage.
</Arcana>

### Night [#night]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.night}>
  Your **Ω Cast** has **+100 Power** whenever it detonates.

</Arcana>

### The Moon [#moon]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.moon}>
Your **Hex** also charges up automatically as though you used **4** <IconMagick /> every **1 Sec.**

**Awakening**: _Activate **any** surrounding Card. ([Night](/arcana#night), [The Unseen](/arcana#unseen), [Death](/arcana#death))_

</Arcana>

## Row 2

### The Furies [#furies]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.furies}>
  Deal **+30%** damage to foes in your **Casts**.
</Arcana>

### The Titan [#titan]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.titan}>
  Gain **+40** <IconMaxLife /> and **+40** <IconMaxMagick />.
</Arcana>

### The Messenger [#messenger]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.messenger}>
You have **+5%** chance to **Dodge**.

</Arcana>

### The Unseen [#unseen]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.unseen}>
  Restore **6** <IconMagick /> every **1 Sec.**
</Arcana>

### Death [#death]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.death}>
  Your **Ω Moves** have **+15%** chance to deal **Critical** damage if your last
  one was different.
</Arcana>

## Row 3

### The Swift Runner [#swift-runner]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.swiftRunner}>
  Your **Dash** is immediate and your **Sprint** is **+10%** faster.
</Arcana>

### Eternity [#eternity]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.eternity}>
  Set forth each night with **3** <IconDeathDefiance />.
</Arcana>

Don't leave home without it. As close to a must-pick as you can get.

### The Centaur [#centaur]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.centaur}>
After every **5 Locations**, gain **+5** <IconMaxLife /> and **+5** <IconMaxMagick />.

**Awakening**: _Activate Cards that use **1** <IconGrasp /> through **5** <IconGrasp />._

</Arcana>

### Origination [#origination]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.origination}>
  Deal **+50%** damage to foes afflicted with at least **2 Curse** effects from
  different Olympians.
</Arcana>

### The Lovers [#lovers]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.lovers}>
  In **Guardian Encounters**, you take **0** damage the first **3** time(s) you
  are hit.
</Arcana>

## Row 4

### The Fates [#fates]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.fates}>
  Set forth each night with **+3** <IconReroll />. You can alter **Location
  Rewards**.
</Arcana>

### The Boatman [#boatman]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.boatman}>
  Set forth each night with **+300** <IconGold />.
</Arcana>

### The Artificer [#artificer]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.artificer}>
  Set forth each night with **3** chance(s) to turn any **Minor Find** into a
  random **Major Find**.
</Arcana>

### Excellence [#excellence]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.excellence}>
  Any **Boons** you are offered have **+50%** chance to be improved to
  <TextHighlight type="rare">**Rare**</TextHighlight>.
</Arcana>

### The Queen [#queen]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.queen}>
Any **Boons** you are offered have **+10%** chance to be a <TextHighlight type='duo'>**Duo**</TextHighlight> _(whenever possible)_.

**Awakening**: _Activate no more than **2** Cards that use the same amount of **Grasp**._

</Arcana>

## Row 5

### The Seer [#seer]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.seer}>
  Set forth each night with +4 <IconReroll />.

**Awakening**: _Activate **all** surrounding Cards. ([The Fates](/arcana#the-fates),
[The Boatman](/arcana#the-boatman), [The Champions](/arcana#the-champions))_

</Arcana>

### The Champions [#champions]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.champions}>
  Set forth each night with **+3** <IconReroll />. You can alter **Boons** and
  certain other choices.
</Arcana>

### Strength [#strength]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.strength}>
  While you have no more than **30%** <IconLife />, you take **-50%** damage and
  deal **+50%**.
</Arcana>

### Divinity [#divinity]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.divinity}>
  Any **Boons** you are offered have **+20%** chance to be improved to <TextHighlight type='epic'>**Epic**</TextHighlight>.

**Awakening**: _Activate all **5** Cards in any other row or column._

</Arcana>

### Judgment [#judgment]

<Arcana data={DbArcana.judgment}>
After you vanquish a **Guardian**, activate **5** random inactive **Arcana Cards**.

**Awakening**: _Activate no more than **3** Cards._

</Arcana>
