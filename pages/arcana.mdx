# Arcana Cards

<Callout>All values given are for rank 3.</Callout>

![Arcana Cards](/images/arcana/altar.png)

## Row 1

### The Sorceress

<Arcana
  grasp="1"
  flavor="Left to her own devices, she learned to confront the world on her own terms."
>
  While you **Channel** your **Ω Moves**, everything moves slower for **4 Sec.**
</Arcana>

### The Wayward Son

<Arcana
  grasp="1"
  flavor="Even the Underworld itself struggled to contain him, such was his lust for life"
>
  After you exit a **Location**, restore **4** <IconLife />, or twice that if
  you have no more than **30%** <IconLife />.
</Arcana>

### The Huntress

<Arcana
  grasp="3"
  flavor="Through discipline and instinct comes surpassing skill both in shadow and the light of the Moon."
>
  While you have less than **100%** <IconMagick />, your **Attack** and
  **Special** deal **+50%** damage.
</Arcana>

### Death

<Arcana
  grasp="3"
  flavor="Some mortals would sense his soft approach, others taken by surprise."
>
  Your **Ω Moves** have **+5%** chance to deal **Critical** damage.
</Arcana>

### The Messenger

<Arcana
  grasp='0'
  flavor='With incomparable swiftness can he travel from the highest peaks to the lowest depths.'
>
You have **+10%** chance to **Dodge**.

**Awakening**: _Activate at least **3** Cards that use the same amount of
**Grasp**._

</Arcana>

## Row 2

### The Furies

<Arcana
  grasp='2'
  flavor='In the deepest reaches of the Underworld, three are as feared as the god of the dead himself.'
>
Deal **+30%** damage to foes in your **Casts**.

</Arcana>

### The Titan

<Arcana
  grasp="2"
  flavor="He stole from gods the gift of fire, for mortals; thus he suffered, but he grew strong."
>
  Gain **+40** <IconMaxLife /> and **+40** <IconMaxMagick />.
</Arcana>

### The Unseen

<Arcana
  grasp='5'
  flavor='Upon their ancient oath, in shadow, they serve; and to shadow, they return.'
>
Restore **4** <IconMagick /> every **1 Sec.**

</Arcana>

### Night

<Arcana
  grasp="4"
  flavor="She sprang from Chaos, and formed much of the natural world, above the surface and below."
>
  Your **Hex** also charges up automatically as though you used **4**
  <IconMagick /> every **1 Sec.**
</Arcana>

### The Lovers

<Arcana
  grasp="2"
  flavor="Their music brought them together, and not even Death could keep them apart for long."
>
  In **Guardian Encounters**, you take **0** damage the first **3** time(s) you
  are hit.
</Arcana>

## Row 3

### The Swift Runner

<Arcana
  grasp="1"
  flavor="Those who attempted to confront the greatest of the Greeks merely met his spear."
>
  Your **Dash** is immediate and your **Sprint** is **+10%** faster.
</Arcana>

### Eternity

<Arcana
  grasp="4"
  flavor="The inevitable cannot be prevented, though perhaps can be forestalled."
>
  Set forth each night with **3** <IconDeathDefiance />.
</Arcana>
Don't leave home without it. As close to a must-pick as you can get.

### The Centaur

<Arcana
  grasp='0'
  flavor='Many of the greatest heroes mortalkind has to offer have in common his patient instruction.'
>
After every **5 Locations**, gain **+5** <IconMaxLife /> and **+5** <IconMaxMagick />.

**Awakening**: _Activate Cards that use **1 Grasp** through **5 Grasp**._

</Arcana>

### The Moon

<Arcana
  grasp='3'
  flavor='Even as she races her steeds across the fathomless sky, she bears witness to all.'
>
Your **Ω Cast** has **+100 Power** whenever it detonates.

</Arcana>

### Strength

<Arcana
  grasp="5"
  flavor="The power to surpass the might of the gods is one that must always be held in check."
>
  While you have no more than **30%** <IconLife />, you take
  **-50%** damage and deal **+50%**.
</Arcana>

## Row 4

### The Fates

<Arcana
  grasp="3"
  flavor="Even the gods themselves are subject to the whims of the three weavers of destiny."
>
  Set forth each night with **+3** <IconReroll />. You can alter **Location
  Rewards**.
</Arcana>

### The Boatman

<Arcana
  grasp="5"
  flavor="Vast riches lie hidden in the realm of the dead; but only a modest fee is needed to get in."
>
  Set forth each night with **+300**
  <IconGold />.
</Arcana>

### Origination

<Arcana
  grasp="3"
  flavor="From the infinite void emerged all that exists, and back to that void it all may yet return."
>
  Deal **+50%** damage to foes afflicted with at least **2 Curse** effects.
</Arcana>

### Excellence

<Arcana
  grasp="5"
  flavor="Not even gods can always live to their fullest purpose; yet mortals all must strive."
>
  Any **Boons** you are offered have **+50%** chance to be improved to
  <TextHighlight type="rare">**Rare**</TextHighlight>.
</Arcana>

### The Queen

<Arcana
  grasp='0'
  flavor='From Olympus she descends to reign in the Underworld, as the surface-land grows cold.'
>
  Any **Boons** you are offered have **+10%** chance to be a
  <TextHighlight type='duo'>**Duo**</TextHighlight> _(whenever possible)_.

    **Awakening**: _Activate no more than **2** Cards that use the same amount of **Grasp**._

</Arcana>

## Row 5

### The Seer

<Arcana
  grasp='0'
  flavor='Having been stricken blind, she saw much more than any mortal could.'
>
  Set forth each night with +4 <IconReroll />.

**Awakening**: _Activate each surrounding Card. ([The Fates](/arcana#the-fates),
[The Boatman](/arcana#the-boatman), [The Champions](/arcana#the-champions))_

</Arcana>

### The Champions

<Arcana
  grasp="4"
  flavor="Once-mortal foes, they found each other once again in death, and were exalted in Elysium."
>
  Set forth each night with **+3** <IconReroll />. You can alter **Boons** and
  certain other choices.
</Arcana>

### The Artificer

<Arcana
  grasp="6"
  flavor="Few can turn raw materials into works of extraordinary genius; none as can he."
>
  Set forth each night with **3** chance(s) to turn any **Minor Find** into a
  random **Major Find**.
</Arcana>

### Divinity

<Arcana
  grasp='0'
  flavor='The privilege of godhood is bestowed at birth, save for the rarest cases, when it is earned instead.'
>
  Any **Boons** you are offered have **+20%** chance to be improved to
  <TextHighlight type='epic'>**Epic**</TextHighlight>.

    **Awakening**: _Activate all **5** Cards in any other row._

</Arcana>

### Judgment

<Arcana
  grasp='0'
  flavor='The Underworld King is to decide what shall become of each and every mortal life.'
>
After you vanquish a **Guardian**, activate **5** random inactive **Arcana Cards**.

**Awakening**: _Activate no more than **3** Cards._

</Arcana>
